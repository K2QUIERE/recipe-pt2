Spaghetti with Meat Sauce

This is a simple, quick and delicious spaghetti recipe that is a must have for any pasta lover.With a blend of spices from oregano to basil and olive oil, you'll be sure to turn heads at the next family gathering!

Ingredients

  • 200g spaghetti
  • 400g ground beef
  • 1 can (400g) crushed tomatoes
  • 2 cloves garlic
  • 1 onion
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Olive oil

Instructions

  1. Cook the spaghetti according to package instructions.
  2. In a pan, heat olive oil and saut√© chopped onion and minced garlic until soft.
  3. Add ground beef and cook until browned.
  4. Stir in crushed tomatoes, salt, and pepper. Simmer for about 10 minutes.
  5. Drain the spaghetti and mix with the sauce.
  6. Serve hot, garnished with grated cheese if desired.
